Password Guide

Creating strong, unique passwords is essential for protecting your online accounts. Here are some tips to help you create and manage secure passwords.

Password Strength Checker

Enter a password to test its strength:

✕ At least 8 characters

✕ Contains a lowercase letter

✕ Contains an uppercase letter

✕ Contains a number

✕ Contains a special character (!, @, #, $...)

✕ At least 12 characters (recommended)

Creating Stronger Passwords

  • At least 12 characters long
  • Mix of uppercase and lowercase letters
  • Includes numbers and special characters
  • Not based on personal information
  • Unique - not reused across multiple accounts
