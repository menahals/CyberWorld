Cybersecurity News

Stay updated with the latest real cybersecurity incidents and breaches from around the world.

Recent Incidents

Data Breach ⚠ High

Telus Data Breach: 700TB of Data Stolen

March 2026

The ShinyHunters hacking group claimed responsibility for breaching Canadian telecom firm Telus, stealing personally identifiable information, call data, background check details, and source code.

Security Insights: Always encrypt sensitive data and train employees to recognise social engineering attacks.

Ransomware ⚠ Critical

Ransomware Attack on Minot Water Treatment Plant

April 2026

A ransomware attack hit Minot's Water Treatment Plant in North Dakota, forcing the facility to revert to manual management processes and disrupting normal operations.

Security Insights: Keep critical systems isolated from the internet and maintain regular offline backups.

Data Breach ⚠ High

Panera Bread: 5.1 Million Accounts Exposed

January 2026

ShinyHunters claimed theft of Panera Bread customer data and leaked a 760MB archive after extortion failed. Names, email addresses, phone numbers, and physical addresses were exposed.

Security Insights: Use unique passwords for every account and enable two-factor authentication to prevent account takeovers.

Ransomware ⚠ Critical

University of Mississippi Medical Center Ransomware Attack

February 2026

A ransomware attack forced the closure of all 35 clinic locations statewide and led to the cancellation of scheduled appointments and elective surgeries.

Security Insights: Split your network and keep offline backups so ransomware cannot spread and data can be restored.

Data Breach ⚠ Medium

European Commission Cloud Infrastructure Attacked

March 24, 2026

A cyberattack struck the cloud infrastructure hosting the Europa web platform. Early findings indicated data was taken from affected websites, though the Commission said the incident was contained within nine hours.

Security Insights: Regularly test cloud environments for vulnerabilities and have a fast incident response plan ready.

Data Breach ⚠ High

France National Bank Account Registry Breach

February 2026

France's Ministry of Economy disclosed that a hacker used stolen credentials to access the national bank account registry, exposing data tied to approximately 1.2 million accounts.

Security Insights: Enable multi-factor authentication on all accounts, especially those with access to sensitive financial data.
