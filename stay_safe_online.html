Stay Safe Online

Practical tips and best practices to protect your personal data and maintain your digital security while navigating the online world.

Password Security

  1. Use strong, unique passwords for each account.
  2. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) whenever possible.
  3. Avoid sharing passwords and use a reputable password manager.
  4. Change passwords regularly.

Safe Browsing Practices

  1. Keep your browser and plugins up to date.
  2. Be cautious when clicking on links or downloading attachments.
  3. Use a reputable antivirus program and keep it updated.
  4. Avoid visiting suspicious or untrusted websites.

Public WiFi Safety

  1. Forget networks after use.
  2. Avoid accessing sensitive accounts or performing financial transactions.
  3. Ensure your device's firewall is enabled.
  4. Keep your device's software updated.

Personal Data Protection

  1. Don't overshare personal information online.
  2. Verify requests for personal information.
  3. Review your privacy settings on social media platforms.
  4. Shred documents containing sensitive information before disposal.

Device Security

  1. Keep operating systems and apps updated.
  2. Use screen and biometric authentication.
  3. Install apps from official sources only.
  4. Backup important data regularly
